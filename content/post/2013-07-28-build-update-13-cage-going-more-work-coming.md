It&#8217;s been almost 3 weeks since the last update so we must have made progress, right? <!--more-->Well, some of us did. The cage is really close to done and the motor probably is, although we&#8217;ll know more when we actually speak to the motor builder. That should happen tomorrow. The turbo and 32mm restrictor were removed from what they were normally attached to and delivered to Rally/Race so we can get them attached together and then inspected/wired. The bushings are starting to go into the sub-frame and the front sub-frame was POR-15&#8217;d (which seems to have worked quite well) so things are starting to come together there. But there&#8217;s still the impeding time suck of attacking the rebuild, paint, etc. It&#8217;ll be fun, but it will also be a lot of work. And probably pictures and stories. There will be blood.

In happier news &#8211; pictures!
